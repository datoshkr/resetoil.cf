ACURA Integra 1995

1. Turn ignition to (II) - do not start the engine.

2. Press and hold the RESET button located below and to the right of the steering column below the the dashboard for more than 3 seconds until the reminder light turns off.

3. Turn the ignition off and then start the engine to verify the indicator has been reset.
