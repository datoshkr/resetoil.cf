ACURA Integra 1988

1. Reset the indicator by inserting the key in the slot below the indicator.

Or

1. Turn ignition to (II) - do not start the engine.

2. Press and hold the RESET button on the bottom of the dash for more than 3 seconds until the reminder light turns off.

3. Turn the ignition off and then start the engine to verify the indicator has been reset.
