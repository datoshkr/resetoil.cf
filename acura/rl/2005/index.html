ACURA RL 2005

1. Turn ignition to (II) - do not start the engine.

2. Press the SELECT/RESET button until the oil life displays.

3. Press and hold the SELECT/RESET button for 10 seconds.

4. Press the SELECT/RESET button to select OK to reset the display.

5. Select RESET on the display and press the SELECT/RESET button.

6. Turn the ignition off and then start the engine to verify the indicator has been reset.
