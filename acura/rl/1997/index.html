ACURA RL 1997

1. Turn the key to off position.

2. Press and hold the SELECT and RESET buttons on the instrument panel and turn the ignition to position (II) - do not start the engine.

3. Hold the SELECT and RESET buttons until the indicator resets.

4. Turn the ignition off and then start the engine to verify the indicator has been reset.
