ACURA CSX 2011

1. Turn the ignition to (II) - do not start the engine.

2. Press the SEL/RESET button to the left of the steering wheel repeatedly until the engine oil life appears.

3. Press and hold the SEL/RESET button for more than 10 seconds until the engine oil life blinks.

4. Press the SEL/RESET button again for more than 5 seconds and the engine oil life will reset to 100.

5. Turn the ignition off and then start the engine to verify the indicator has been reset.
